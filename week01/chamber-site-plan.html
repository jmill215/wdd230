Park City Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Overview

Site Name

The site name I chose was Park City Chamber of Commerce. The reason I selected park city specifically was due to being familiar with the cities in Utah that were more tourist tracked, of which Park City was the one that stuck out.

Site Purpose

The Park City Chamber of Commerce acts as a central community for businesses in the area, and will hold events and actitivies to promote economic growth for the city.

Scenarios

  • What type of events does the Chamber hold to promote business growth and networking in the area?
  • Where could I find, if any, statistics on the population or economic growth of the area?
  • Where can I find the locations of said events?
